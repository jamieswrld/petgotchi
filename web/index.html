How to earn $GOTCHI

Play-to-earn — care for your Gotchi to earn tokens.

  1. Connect a wallet (or play as a guest to try it out).
  2. Care for your Gotchi — feed, clean, heal & play.
  3. Catch coins in the mini-game: +5 $GOTCHI each — 2 plays/day.
  4. Every action earns XP. 100 XP = +1 Level.
  5. Hit a milestone level to unlock a claim reward.
  6. Press Claim to bank your $GOTCHI.
